About

Myself

My name is Popovici Nina Andreea, I did taste art since early childhood when I started to imagine and draw the bedtime stories told by my parents and grandparents. At the beginning I have started small, on scrap paper, then in one day, exhausting all the pages, I was looking at the walls in my room, then with a smile and exploratory heart I dared to expand my visions on those 4 immaculate “canvases” . I have created my own virtual world, timeless, with my heroes, unseen creatures and surreal, all imagined, deformed and transformed by my own understanding and imagination ... I have been in love with drawing ever since, as it offered me inner peace, smiles and serenity, things that the majority of us are craving. I am currently studying Multimedia Design and Communication at KEA University in Copnehagen, Denmark. This portfolio is a summary of my best work of the first semester.

Services

What I can do for you

Web Design

My process of creation begins with paper and pencil. I like to create many different layouts for my websites. I often get inspired from other websites that catch my imagination and interest. I look for inspiration in very colourfull and dense designs but in this case I tryed to keep it as simple and professional as possible. Everything can be found on the main page, all the content can be found on the same page and can be reached as you scroll further down. I have a dark navy blue as a dominant colour, even tho I like picture backgrounds because they are more fluid and create an atmosphere of mistery. I added a playfull mix of yellow and blue for my highlights and links. The mobile has been designed mainly for mobile use, but can be viewed on a desktop and Ipad also.

  • Digital Print Operator

    Digital print operators use computers to create layouts and printed materials that are sent to printing presses for production. Their job is to listen to client specifications and adjust attributes such as size and resolution to create a high-quality finished product. People who have a strong eye for detail and who enjoy working with graphic design and technology can thrive as digital print operators. Every day, they manage multiple projects with varying timelines. Digital print operators can work full or part time and are typically employed in the commercial printing, marketing, and publishing industries.
