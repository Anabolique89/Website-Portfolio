Fronter Prototype

"This is our design process for the "Fronter Prototype" group assignment. This was just a concept idea, for another improved version of the "Fronter" program, that would work as a mobile application. Keeping the original structure of the program but adding some new features like a chat room where students can reach others faster, through the study platform.

"Number 1" Team

Work Process

Steps and Ideas

  • Sketching

    Imagining different ideas and concepts, how they would work together and simplify the life of the user. Pen and paper, markers and a good team is all that is needed for a successful outcome.

  • Testing The Concept

    After coming up with a few ideas from the previous day of the sprint, looking over the sketches with a fresh eye and a cool mind is a must, in order to pick the most efficient and simple option and manifest it into existance!

  • Design XD Prototype

    The design process itself is taking place. We use Adobe XD and sometimes Photoshop.

  • Reviews

    Presenting your design and layout ideas to your team mates and to an array of experts - helps improve your future performance and helps you see the flaws of your project, which is always needed in UX design.

  • Link To Prototype

    The big day of launching the web prototype and presenting the final version to your desired adience.
